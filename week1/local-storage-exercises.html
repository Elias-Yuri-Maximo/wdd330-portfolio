Mobile First

Nowadays we have a wide range of devices. and most people don't have a desktop computer most people will use my websites thorugh a mobile phone.

People usually don't know what they want until I show it to them. Provide what the client wants as fast as possible.

Reachability matters. Make sure that things are in reach. The fastest things load the bigger the retention of users.

Network affects the speed. Most people in the world have access only to 3g.

Things to do to reduce speed

  • Reduce image usage
  • SVG and webfonts are lower bandwith
  • Optimize Js and CSS files (reduce white space)
  • GZIP files to eliminate unecessary data

We need to be empathetic to user's needs. Primary controls. Put them as close as possible to the user. (Bottom of the screen). Reachability creates a sense of speed. Don't use the hamburger menu.

Forms: focus on the important things first. Make the user think less. Forms ask too much from information from the user. Reduce cognitive load. Float labels. Present the numbers with a number pad.
Make things as easy as possible for the users.
